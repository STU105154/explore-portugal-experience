Privacy & Cookies

We respect your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how cookies are used on our website.

What we collect

When you contact us or book a service, you may share information such as your name, phone number, email address, pick-up location, dates and service preferences.

We only collect what is necessary to respond to your request and provide the service.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • reply to enquiries and confirm bookings;
  • provide tours and transfers safely and professionally;
  • send important service-related messages (e.g., timing, pick-up details, changes).

We do not sell your personal data.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use cookies to keep the website reliable and to understand basic traffic (so we can improve the experience).

Cookie choices

You can choose:

  • Accept all — enables analytics cookies (helps us understand traffic and improve pages).
  • Essential only — keeps only the cookies needed for the website to work properly.

If you want to change your choice later, you can do it here at any time.
