Services & bookings

How do I request a quote? +

Send a WhatsApp message, email, or use the booking form. The best request includes dates, approximate times, number of people, and pick-up / drop-off locations.

We reply with a clear price and simple conditions — in writing.

How far in advance should I book? +

The earlier the better, especially during high season and holidays. When possible, we still try to accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.

Do you do transfers outside Lisbon? +

Yes. We’re based in Lisbon, but we provide transfers and full-day services across Portugal (Porto, Algarve, Alentejo, Central regions, etc.). Pricing depends on distance and total time.

On the day

Where do I meet the driver at the airport? +

We confirm the meeting point in writing before the service (for example, arrivals area near a specific reference). If a name sign is included, the agreed name will be visible at the exit.

Do you monitor flights if there is a delay? +

Yes. For airport services, we monitor the flight and adjust pick-up time. If delays are very long, we’ll message you to confirm the best solution for both sides.

Can we change the route during the day? +

Within reason, yes. On tours and hourly services you can request small changes, add a stop, or adjust the pace. If changes significantly affect time or distance, we discuss it first.

Payments & cancellations

Which payment methods do you accept? +

Depending on the service and lead time, options may include MB Way, Revolut, PayPal, or another agreed method. The payment method is always confirmed in writing before booking.

Do you require a deposit? +

For longer services, high-demand dates or special requests, a deposit may be required. The amount and conditions are clearly stated in the proposal before any commitment.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Cancellation terms depend on the service type and notice period. When possible, we prioritize rescheduling first. Exact conditions are always sent with each proposal.

Other questions

Do you speak languages other than Portuguese? +

Yes. We communicate in Portuguese and English, and often understand Spanish and French. If you have a specific language need, mention it in your request.

Can you provide a child seat? +

When requested in advance, a child seat may be provided depending on the age/weight details you share at the time of booking.

What if my question isn’t here? +

Send a simple WhatsApp message or email. Often a short reply is enough to confirm if the service fits what you need.
